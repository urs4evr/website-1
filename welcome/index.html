Timer & Daily Limits

Set daily time limits for any website. A visual countdown timer shows your remaining time with color-coded warnings as time runs out. Stay aware of your browsing habits and make conscious decisions about how you spend your time online.

Social Media Cleaner

Remove distracting elements from social media platforms. Hide shorts, comments, recommendations, and more on YouTube, Instagram, Twitter, and other platforms. Focus on what matters without the endless scroll.

Analytics & Insights

Track your browsing habits with detailed charts and visualizations. See daily, weekly, and monthly trends to understand your productivity patterns. Identify your most visited sites and time spent to make informed decisions.

Impulse Check

Take a mindful pause before accessing distracting sites. A breathing exercise helps you make conscious decisions about your time. Break the habit of mindless browsing with this gentle reminder to check in with yourself.

Smart Blacklist

Completely block distracting websites when you need to focus. Get motivated with custom quotes and affirmations when you try to access blocked sites. Create a distraction-free environment for deep work.

Time Restrictions

Schedule when your limits should apply throughout the day. Set work hours, break times, or weekend rules to match your routine. Have full control over when productivity features are active.
